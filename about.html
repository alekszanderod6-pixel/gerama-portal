Our History

Team GERAMA started in 2023 with 5 students (Gad, Edem, Aaron, Rashida, Mawuli) as a small group study. Later, Aleks joined, and the name evolved from GERMA to GERAMA. The study group grew rapidly, especially before exams, attracting many classmates. By L400 (2025), the group had become a well‑known academic community. After graduation, the founding members ensured the legacy continued, with batch leaders carrying forward the mission. Today, GERAMA is a large community across all levels of engineering at UENR.
