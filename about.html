Our History

Team GERAMA started somewhere April 2022 with 4 students (Gad, Edem, Rashida, and Mawuli ) as a small study group, birthing the name TEAM GERM. Later in the year, Aaron joined, allowing the name change to be TEAM GERMA and then the name evolved from GERMA to GERAMA when Aleks also joined. The study group grew rapidly, especially before exams, attracting many classmates. By L400 (2025), the group had become a well‑known academic community. After graduation, the founding members ensured the legacy continued, with batch leaders carrying forward the mission. Today, GERAMA is a large community across all levels of engineering at UENR.
